Things to Do:

  • Go Parks
  • Hiking
  • Visit Museums

Local Tips:

  • Travel Documents: Ensure you have all the necessary travel documents and check visa requirements before your trip.
  • Language: Urdu is the official language, but English is widely spoken, especially in urban areas. Learning a few basic Urdu phrases can be helpful.
  • Transportation: Islamabad has a well-organized public transportation system, including taxis and ride-sharing services. Negotiate taxi fares before starting the ride if the meter is not used
